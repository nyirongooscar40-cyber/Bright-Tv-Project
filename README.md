# Bright-Tv-Project

 **Dataset Overview:**
User Profiles: Demographic and subscription details of BrightTV users.
Viewer Transactions: Session-level consumption records (each viewing session logged as one record).
Time Zone Note: All timestamps are provided in UTC and must be converted to South Africa Standard Time (SAST) for analysis.

**Deliverables:**
A 20-minute presentation covering the following key areas:

1. User & Usage Trends
Subscriber growth patterns
Viewing behavior across demographics, devices, and times of day
Peak vs. low consumption periods

2. Consumption Drivers
Factors influencing engagement (e.g., content type, time of week, device usage, demographics)
Correlation between subscription tiers and consumption

3. Content Recommendations
Identify genres or formats that boost engagement
Suggest targeted content strategies for low-consumption days

4. Growth Initiatives
Marketing and promotional strategies to attract new users
Retention tactics to reduce churn
Partnerships, bundles, or loyalty programs to expand reach

 **Additional Data & Insights:**
External benchmarks from streaming industry trends
Comparative analysis of competitor strategies
Predictive modeling to forecast subscription growth under different initiatives

**Tools & Methods:**
Data cleaning and transformation (UTC → SAST conversion)
Exploratory data analysis (EDA) for trends and correlations
Visualization dashboards for clear storytelling

Recommendation framework for CVM team
